Burrito Time!

Now, what does it take to make a good burrito?

POST POST POST POST
First things first: get yourself a good ol' tortilla. The most common types are corn and flour. And don't you ever forget the main protein filling! Pork, egg, fish, beef, and more... it's all good! The toppings are everything. Get some lettuce, a little pico de gallo, maybe a bag of cheese, tomato... the options are endless! Finally, of course, who could fotget to have a delectable taco sauce. Maybe it's just red or green chile, maybe it's a sweet or spicy salsa.
